CFA Equity Research Challenge 2024

As a finalist in the CFA Equity Research Challenge 2024 France, I worked with a team to prepare a comprehensive buy-side initiating coverage report on Amundi. Our analysis included detailed financial modeling, valuation, and strategic recommendations.

This project was conducted under the supervision of EMLYON Business School, where we developed a thorough equity research report on Amundi, France's largest asset management company. Our work involved in-depth financial analysis, industry research, and valuation methodologies to provide a well-founded investment recommendation.
