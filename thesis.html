Market Risk Forecasting: Traditional vs. Machine Learning Approaches

This thesis investigates whether machine learning and hybrid models can outperform traditional risk forecasting techniques like Value-at-Risk (VaR), particularly during market crises. Drawing on my experience at Rothschild & Co, I compare different models in terms of accuracy, robustness under stress, and interpretability using SHAP and LIME.
